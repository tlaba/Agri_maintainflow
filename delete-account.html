Delete your account & data

MaintainFlow Ag · Last updated: 1 July 2026

You can delete your MaintainFlow Ag account and its associated data at any time. This page explains how, and what is removed.

Option 1 — delete it yourself in the app

  1. Open MaintainFlow Ag and sign in.
  2. Go to More → your account → Delete account.
  3. Confirm. Your account and synced records are permanently removed.

Option 2 — request deletion by email

If you can't access the app, email info@maintainflow.pro from the email address on your account (or include the phone number you signed up with) and ask us to delete your account. We'll action it and confirm.

What gets deleted

  • Your account (email or phone sign-in).
  • All farm records synced to your account — fields, herds, tasks, expenses, yields, equipment and notes.
  • Any business listing you published to the Agri-services directory.

Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Synced data is removed within 30 days of the request; anonymous, aggregated analytics that cannot identify you may be retained. Payment records held by our payment processor (Stripe) may be retained where required by law.

Data stored only on your device

If you used the app without an account, your records are stored locally on your phone and were never uploaded. Remove them by clearing the app's data / site storage, or uninstalling the app.

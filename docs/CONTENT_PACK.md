### Mon · 1:00 PM ET — Tip
![Beat the frost](social/tip-beat-the-frost.png)
> ❄️ Beat the frost this winter.
>
> A few moves that save your crop:
> 🧥 Cover seedlings with frost cloth or row covers
> 🥬 Cabbage & kale handle light frost — tomatoes & peppers won't
> 💧 Don't overwater: cold + wet soil = root rot
> 🐛 Keep scouting for cutworm, slugs & armyworm
>
> Protect now, harvest later. 🌱
>
> 💬 How cold does it get on your farm? 👇

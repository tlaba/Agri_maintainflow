# MaintainFlow Ag — Marketing Strategy

_Last updated: 2026-06-19 · Owner: Growth_

> A practical, offline-first farm-management app for African growers.
> One app for **crops, livestock, money and maintenance** — built for real
> phones, real networks, and real farm economics.

---

## 1. Where we are

MaintainFlow Ag is a Progressive Web App (installable, works offline) currently
covering:

- **Crops** — fields, work orders (spray/fertilizer/irrigation), spray
  re-entry windows, weather + frost alerts.
- **Livestock** _(new in v1.4.0)_ — herds (cattle, goats, sheep, poultry,
  pigs), health/vaccination schedules, weights & growth, milk/egg production,
  and one-tap event logging (births, deaths, sales, purchases, treatments).
- **Money** — expense tracking, cost-per-hectare, spend breakdowns, local
  market prices (Pula).
- **Equipment** — service/maintenance reminders for machinery.
- **Pro** — yield & herd analytics, lender-ready credit summary + PDF export,
  input-supplier marketplace.

**Positioning today is wider than "crop app".** The v1.4.0 livestock release is
our chance to re-introduce the product as a *whole-farm* tool — which is how
most of our market actually farms (mixed crop + livestock).

## 2. Positioning

**One-liner:**
> Run your whole farm from your pocket — crops, livestock, money and
> maintenance, even offline.

**Category:** Smallholder & emerging-commercial farm management (Africa-first).

**Positioning statement:**
> For African farmers who juggle crops *and* animals on notebooks and memory,
> MaintainFlow Ag is the offline-first phone app that turns daily farm activity
> into records that cut costs and unlock finance — unlike spreadsheets or
> imported Western farm software, it works on cheap phones with no signal, in
> local currency, and speaks the language of how Africans actually farm.

**Three pillars (everything ladders up to these):**

1. **Whole-farm, one app.** Crops + livestock + money + machinery in a single
   place. No app-switching, no second notebook.
2. **Works where you farm.** Offline-first, installs like an app, light on
   data and battery, local currency and greetings.
3. **Records that pay you back.** Logging turns into cost savings, better
   yields, and a **lender-ready credit profile** — financial inclusion is the
   long-game wedge.

## 3. Target market & segments

**Primary geography:** Botswana (home market — Pula pricing, Setswana UX, local
market prices), then expand across SADC / Sub-Saharan Africa.

### Ideal Customer Profiles (ICPs)

| # | Segment | Who | Core job-to-be-done | Why us |
|---|---------|-----|---------------------|--------|
| **A** | **Emerging commercial farmer** | 10–200 ha and/or 30–300 head; semi-formal; wants to grow & borrow | Prove the farm is bankable; control input costs | Lender summary + analytics + cost/ha |
| **B** | **Mixed smallholder** | Few ha + a herd/flock; family-run; intermittent signal | Stop losing track of tasks, treatments, money | Offline, simple, whole-farm |
| **C** | **Livestock-first keeper** | Cattle/goats/poultry primary income | Herd health, mortality, weights, sales | New livestock module is the hook |
| **D** | **Agribusiness enablers** _(B2B2C)_ | Co-ops, lenders/MFIs, input suppliers, extension officers, insurers | Reach & de-risk many farmers at once | We generate the records they need |

**Beachhead:** Segments **A + C** in Botswana — they feel the pain hardest, have
the most to gain (credit, cost control), and the livestock launch speaks
directly to them.

## 4. Value proposition by audience

- **Farmer:** "Never lose a record again. See exactly where money goes. Get a
  report banks actually accept."
- **Lender / MFI / insurer:** "Verifiable, structured farm records and a
  credit-readiness score — lower the cost of assessing smallholders."
- **Input supplier / co-op:** "A warm channel to active farmers at the moment
  they're planning purchases."

---

## 5. Pricing & monetization

- **Freemium.** Free tier covers daily management (fields, herds, tasks,
  expenses, equipment) — this is the habit-forming core and our growth engine.
- **Pro** unlocks the *outcomes*: analytics, lender PDF report, input
  marketplace. Price in **Pula**, framed against ROI ("one approved input loan
  or one avoided mortality pays for a year").
- **Future revenue lines (post product-market fit):**
  - **B2B2C distribution fees** — lenders/co-ops/suppliers sponsor or bulk-buy
    Pro for their farmers.
  - **Marketplace take-rate / lead-gen** from input suppliers.
  - **Anonymized, consented benchmarks** (yield/cost) for agribusiness — strict
    opt-in, never raw farmer data.

**Guiding principle:** monetize *value created* (finance, savings, sales), not
basic record-keeping. Keep the free tier genuinely useful.

## 6. Go-to-market motions

We run three motions in parallel, weighted by stage.

### 6.1 Product-led (always-on)
- Free, install-in-one-tap PWA; works offline so it spreads in low-connectivity
  areas.
- In-app **"back up your farm / sign in"** and **share** prompts.
- Seeded demo farm so the value is obvious in 30 seconds.
- Lightweight **referral**: farmers invite neighbours (offline word-of-mouth is
  the dominant channel in this market — make it easy to show, not just send).

### 6.2 Community & field (primary for trust)
- **Extension officers & lead farmers** as champions — train-the-trainer.
- **Demo days / farmer field days, auctions, cattle posts, co-op meetings** —
  this is where mixed farmers gather; do live phone demos.
- **Local radio + WhatsApp groups** (dominant in rural Botswana) for tips +
  app nudges.

### 6.3 Partnerships (force-multiplier, highest leverage)
- **Lenders / MFIs / NDB-type institutions & insurers** — co-design the lender
  report; they push the app to applicants because it lowers their assessment
  cost.
- **Co-operatives & input suppliers** — bundle Pro, feature them in-app.
- **Government / donor agriculture programmes & NGOs** — distribution at scale;
  records help them measure impact.

## 7. The hero campaign: "Now your animals count too" (livestock launch)

The v1.4.0 livestock release is the flagship campaign for the next quarter.

- **Message:** _"MaintainFlow now tracks your livestock too — herd health,
  weights, production and sales, all offline."_
- **Assets (ready / in progress):** 15s feature reels (9:16, 1:1, 16:9) with
  captions + music, 10 feature screenshots, Facebook cover banner. Repurpose
  into WhatsApp-status clips, radio reads, and a one-page flyer for field days.
- **Hook order in every asset:** whole-farm → livestock is new → records →
  finance.
- **Proof:** short farmer testimonial videos (mortality avoided, loan secured,
  costs cut) — collect from beachhead users ASAP; nothing converts like a peer.

## 8. Funnel & content

**Funnel:** Reach → Install → Activate (first herd/field + 3 logs) → Habit
(weekly logging) → Upgrade (Pro) → Advocate (refer/testimonial).

| Stage | Goal | Tactics |
|-------|------|---------|
| Reach | Awareness | Reels/Shorts, radio, WhatsApp, field days, partner channels |
| Install | First open | One-tap PWA, QR codes on flyers/banners, demo-day installs |
| Activate | "Aha" in week 1 | Onboarding to add 1 herd + 1 field; seeded demo; nudges |
| Habit | Weekly use | Health/spray reminders, weather/frost alerts, streak-style prompts |
| Upgrade | Pro conversion | Lender report preview, analytics teaser, ROI framing |
| Advocate | Referrals | Easy share, referral reward, testimonial capture |

**Content pillars:** (1) practical farming tips (seasonal, crop+livestock),
(2) "money & finance" literacy (cost/ha, getting loan-ready), (3) product
how-tos, (4) farmer stories. Format-first for **WhatsApp + short video + radio**,
not blog posts.

---

## 9. Channel priority (effort vs. payoff)

1. **Partnerships** (lenders, co-ops, extension) — highest leverage, build now.
2. **Field/community** (demo days, lead farmers, radio, WhatsApp) — trust + installs.
3. **Product-led/referral** — compounding, low cost.
4. **Paid social** (Facebook/Instagram, geo-targeted) — use sparingly to amplify
   the livestock campaign and retarget installers; not the core engine.

## 10. 90-day plan

**Days 0–30 — Launch livestock & instrument.**
- Ship the livestock campaign (reels, banner, flyer, radio read).
- Add/verify analytics for the funnel events above (install, activate, log,
  upgrade, refer).
- Recruit 15–25 beachhead farmers (Segments A+C); start testimonial capture.
- Open conversations with 2–3 lenders/co-ops about the lender report.

**Days 31–60 — Prove activation & one partner.**
- Run 3–5 field days / co-op demos; measure install→activate.
- Sign 1 pilot partner (lender or co-op) to push the app to their farmers.
- Tighten onboarding to lift week-1 activation; ship 2–3 testimonial clips.

**Days 61–90 — Scale what works & convert.**
- Double down on the best channel; first geo-targeted paid burst.
- Push Pro conversion via lender-report value; report on KPIs vs. targets.
- Decide next-market / next-feature based on data.

## 11. KPIs (north star + supporting)

- **North star:** Weekly Active Farms logging real activity (records created/week).
- Installs and **install→activation rate** (≥ X% add a herd/field + 3 logs in wk 1).
- **W4 retention** (still logging at 4 weeks).
- **Free→Pro conversion** and Pro retention.
- Lender reports generated; partner-sourced installs.
- Referral coefficient (invites → installs).
- CAC by channel vs. LTV (target LTV:CAC ≥ 3:1 once paid is on).

_(Set hard numeric targets after 30 days of clean baseline data.)_

## 12. Risks & mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| Low digital literacy / trust | Field demos, lead-farmer champions, dead-simple UX, local language |
| Connectivity & device limits | Offline-first PWA, light footprint — already a core strength |
| "Why pay?" objection | Keep free tier valuable; price Pro on finance/savings ROI |
| Slow paid acquisition in rural areas | Lead with partnerships + community, not paid |
| Data trust / privacy | Explicit consent, farmer owns data, anonymized benchmarks only opt-in |
| Single-market dependence | Validate in Botswana, design for SADC currencies/locales from day one |

## 13. Messaging cheat-sheet

- **Tagline:** _Run your whole farm from your pocket._
- **Livestock hook:** _Now your animals count too._
- **Finance hook:** _Turn your records into a report banks accept._
- **Always include:** works **offline**, **free to start**, **Pula** pricing.
- **Tone:** practical, respectful, peer-to-peer — never preachy or "Silicon
  Valley". Show real farms, real animals, real phones.

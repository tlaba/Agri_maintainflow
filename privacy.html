Privacy Policy

MaintainFlow Ag · Last updated: 27 June 2026

MaintainFlow Ag ("the app", "we") is a farm-management tool for crops, livestock, costs and equipment. This policy explains what data the app handles and your choices. We designed the app to be offline-first and privacy-respecting: your farm records stay on your device unless you choose to sign in and back them up.

What we collect

  • On your device The farm records you enter — fields, herds, tasks, expenses, yields, equipment and notes — are stored locally on your device (browser storage). If you never sign in, this data never leaves your device.
  • If you sign in (optional) To back up and sync across devices, you may create an account with an email or phone number. Your farm records are then stored in Google Firebase (Firestore) under your account so only you can access them.
  • Community listings (optional) If you publish your business to the Agri-services directory, the details you enter (business name, role, location, country and a contact number) become visible to other users. Don't publish anything you don't want shown publicly.
  • Analytics With your consent, we collect anonymous, cookieless usage events (e.g. which screens are used, whether the app was installed) via Umami to improve the app. This does not identify you and is not sold.
  • Payments (optional) If you buy Pro, payment is processed by Stripe. We do not receive or store your card details.
  • We may store a coarse device locale/country and, only if you tap "use my location", your approximate coordinates to show local weather. Location is never shared.

How we use it

  • To provide the app's features (records, reminders, analytics you asked for, backup/sync).
  • To improve the app using anonymous, consented usage statistics.
  • To process Pro payments and manage your subscription.

We do not sell your personal data or your farm records.

Sharing

We share data only with the service providers needed to run the app: Google Firebase (account & encrypted-in-transit data storage), Stripe (payments), and Umami (anonymous analytics). Community listings you choose to publish are visible to other users by design.

Your choices & rights

  • Use it fully offline without an account — your data stays on your device.
  • Decline analytics at the consent prompt; nothing is sent.
  • Delete your account and data anytime from More → account → Delete account, which removes your synced records. Local data is cleared by removing the app / clearing site data.
  • Remove a community listing you published at any time.

Data retention

Synced records are kept until you delete your account. Anonymous analytics are retained in aggregate. Local data persists on your device until you clear it.

Children

The app is intended for adults managing a farm and is not directed at children.
